## Omni WhatsApp Bot: User Guide

With OmniDub, you can effortlessly translate your voice notes into any language you desire. Follow the simple steps below to start dubbing your voice notes.
<br/>
<div
    style={{
        fontStyle: 'italic',
        fontWeight: 'bold',
    }}
>

</div>

## Step - 1
Visit the [omni](https://www.getomni.app/) platform and navigate to the [tools](https://www.getomni.app/tools) page.<br/>Scroll down to find the ['Dub'](https://www.getomni.app/dub) bot.

    display: 'flex',
    alignItems: 'center',
    justifyContent: 'center',
    marginTop: 30,
}}>
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/BlIY4QH.jpeg" width={500} />
</div>

## Step - 2
Click on the 'WhatsApp' icon to add the bot.

<div style={{
    display: 'flex',
    alignItems: 'center',
    justifyContent: 'center',
    marginTop: 30,
}}>
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/uKrOdUh.jpeg" width={500} />
</div>

After that, you will be navigated to the WhatsApp application and a new chat will be opened with the bot with a default message already loaded.

## Step - 3

### Start a Conversation
- Send the default message i.e., 'Hey! I'd like to dub a video.' to start a conversation with the bot and then follow the guide accordingly.

<div style={{
    display: 'flex',
    alignItems: 'center',
    justifyContent: 'center',
    marginTop: 30,
}}>
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/YJqEj76.jpeg" width={500} />
</div>


### Send a Voice Note
- After that, you will have to send the voice note you need converted to the bot.

<div style={{
    display: 'flex',
    alignItems: 'center',
    justifyContent: 'center',
    marginTop: 30,
}}>
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/rErkROD.jpeg" width={500} />
</div>

### Choose a Language
- The bot will respond with a message giving you a wide range of languages to choose from. That language will be the language of the resultant audio the bot will send to you.

<div style={{
    display: 'flex',
    alignItems: 'center',

    marginTop: 30,

}}>
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/qZURrTC.jpeg" width={500} />
</div>

>For a list of supported languages, click [here](./supported-languages).

### Conversion in Progress
- Once you select a language, the bot will then get to work on converting your voice note. it takes a while to convert your message.
